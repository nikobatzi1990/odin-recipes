Basic Hummus

Image Credit: Joe Lingeman

Description

A very easy snack to make, hummus is both healthy and versatile.
One can easily change the flavor by adding different spices or garnishes.
Hummus is a perfect appetizer to serve at a party or else a nice, light snack
that can be enjoyed between meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups canned chickpeas
  • 2-3 tbsp tahini
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/8 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • cumin & red pepper flakes (to taste)

How to Make

  1. Put chickpeas into blender or food processor; do not drain the liquid out of the can.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and blend well until smooth. Don't let it get too watery!
  3. Enjoy with some crackers or chips!
